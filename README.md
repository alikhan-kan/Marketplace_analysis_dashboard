# Marketplace_analysis_dashboard (Interactive dashboard using MS Power BI)
## Project objective
This particular project is a Power BI dashboard created as part of my portfolio for Junior Data analyst role
The goal of the project is to analyze sales performance and profitability on marketplace across different dimensions and demonstrate practical skills in data preprocesing, modeling, and business analysis.

## Business objectives
- Evaluation of business performance
- Revenue tracking
- Profitable items identification
- Customer segment analysis
- Regional contribution
- Identification of managers with negative profit

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Number of customers
- Average check
- Profit Margin %

## Steps applied
- Data import from Web (Google sheets, so the data is relevant)
- Cleaning and Transformation
- Business & Financial analysis
- Visualisation with key metrics

## Insights
- Corporate customers generate the highest profit margins
- Revenue is unevenly distributed across regions, with a few regions driving most sales
- A small number of managers are responsible for disproportionate losses, indicating potential pricing or operational issues.

## Conclusion
Dashboard illustrates key metrics of the business performance with its powerful and negative sides. Visualizations were carefully sorted in order to provide understandable information to potential top-management.
